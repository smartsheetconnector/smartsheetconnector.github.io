Connect your tools

Smartsheet connector is an online extension that allows direct integration between Smartsheet and Atlassian JIRA.

Simply Click a Button

Create a project plan using Smartsheet and directly create tasks in JIRA or just import all your JIRA tasks in your Smartsheet by simply clicking a button.

Easily Sync

Use JIRA for task management and quickly and easily sync between the two systems.

  • Instant Sync

    All changes are synchronized in real-time.

  • Create JIRA tasks automatically

    Get your jira tasks created in no time.

  • Filters

    Set-up filters to define what information you want to flow from Smartsheet to JIRA and the other way around.

  • Custom Field Mapping

    Don’t want to update all fields? Update only the fields you want.

    Be on top of your game without worrying about administrative tasks.

  • User friendly
